Features

Everything you need
to track food spending

A complete toolkit built for households that want real insight into their grocery budget.

🏪

Store Management

Add your favourite supermarkets with a name, address, and logo. See total spend at each store at a glance on beautifully styled store cards.

🛍️

Product Tracking

Log purchases with quantity, unit, price, and date. Session memory remembers your last store and date so repeat entries take seconds — not minutes.

📊

Spending Reports

Week-to-date, month-to-date, and year-to-date totals. Filter by product name, store, or date range. Export to CSV or print in one click.

📈

Price History

Track how prices move over time. Get up/down indicators between purchases and see highest, lowest, and average prices grouped monthly or yearly.

🍽️

Recipe Manager

Build a personal recipe library with ingredients, instructions, nutritional info, and full macro/micronutrient tracking. Filter by cuisine, difficulty, and dietary tags.

📅

Menu Planner

Plan meals ahead by creating menus and adding recipes by course — starter, main, dessert, and more. Filter by occasion and date to stay organised.

How it works

Up and running in minutes

Four simple steps from clone to insight.

1

Create an account

Register securely with email and password. All your data is private and isolated from other users.

2

Add your stores

Set up the supermarkets you shop at. Upload a logo so you can identify them at a glance.

3

Log your purchases

Enter products as you shop. Autocomplete suggests prices and units from past entries to save you time.

4

Explore your data

View reports, analyse price history, and spot trends to make smarter shopping decisions.
